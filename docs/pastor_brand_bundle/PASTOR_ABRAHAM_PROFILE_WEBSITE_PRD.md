# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Title
**Pastor Abraham Tshabuse – Ministry Profile Website**

---

## 1. Purpose
Design and launch a professional profile website for Pastor Abraham Tshabuse, Senior Pastor of Active Faith Church (Ferndale, Randburg), that:

- Presents his calling, philosophy, and leadership journey.
- Showcases the impact and growth of his ministry.
- Positions him credibly for invitations, partnerships, conferences, and inter-church collaboration.
- Complements (not replaces) the existing Active Faith Church website.

This platform should function as a ministry portfolio, leadership profile, and theological positioning statement.

---

## 2. Objectives

### Primary Objectives
1. Establish Pastor Abraham as a credible, thoughtful, visionary church leader.
2. Clearly communicate his theological framework and ministry philosophy.
3. Document the history and structure of Active Faith Church.
4. Provide easy access to sermons, media, and speaking material.
5. Enable other churches and organizations to evaluate and invite him.

### Secondary Objectives
- Improve discoverability via search engines.
- Provide a structured biography for media use.
- Create a digital archive of milestones and achievements.

---

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- Senior pastors
- Church boards
- Conference organizers
- Ministry networks
- Christian institutions

### Secondary Audience
- Church members
- Media professionals
- Prospective partners and donors

---

## 4. Core Messaging

### Central Theme
**“Active Faith – depending on Jesus daily to reach your God-given potential.”**

### Key Positioning Statements
- A pastor who transitioned from geoscience into ministry leadership.
- A structured, governance-minded leader with professional background.

## 11. UI / UX Strategy Options

### Option 1: Executive Leadership Profile (Minimal & Authoritative)
- Clean, whitespace-heavy layout
- Strong typography pairing
- Minimal animation; fast, direct CTAs

### Option 2: Story-Driven Ministry Experience (Narrative)
- Timeline-driven storytelling
- Subtle motion transitions
- Quote overlays and immersive sections

### Option 3: Media-Centric Platform
- Video/podcast-first content discovery
- Category filters + search
- Modular content cards

### Option 4: Hybrid Authority Model (Recommended)
- Authoritative homepage + narrative biography + media library + strong invite funnel

### UX Technical Enhancements
- Mobile-first responsive layout
- Lighthouse target 90+
- Accessibility target WCAG AA
- Structured schema markup (Person, Organization, Sermon)
